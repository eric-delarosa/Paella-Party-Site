Welcome to Paella Party NJ

A Taste of Spain in New Jersey

Chef Diego De La Rosa

Welcome to Paella Party NJ, where we bring the authentic flavors of Spain to your special events. With over 40 years of culinary experience, Chef Diego De La Rosa and his wife Maria have been creating memorable dining experiences for the past decade.

Our paellas are prepared with imported Spanish ingredients and traditional techniques, ensuring an authentic taste that transports you straight to the Mediterranean coast.

  • Authentic Spanish recipes and techniques
  • Premium imported ingredients
  • Prepared fresh on-site at your event
  • Customizable menu options
