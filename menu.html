Authentic Spanish Cuisine

At Paella Party NJ, we take pride in offering authentic Spanish cuisine made with the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Our menu features a variety of paella options, from the classic Valenciana to the dramatic Negra, as well as a selection of Spanish tapas and other specialties.

All of our dishes are prepared fresh on-site by Chef Diego, ensuring the highest quality and most authentic flavors. We can customize our menu to accommodate your preferences and dietary requirements, making your event truly special.

Dietary Information

We understand that many guests have specific dietary requirements. We are happy to accommodate the following dietary needs with advance notice:

  • Vegetarian: Our Vegetarian Paella and many of our tapas are suitable for vegetarians.
  • Vegan: We can prepare vegan versions of our Vegetarian Paella and select tapas upon request.
  • Gluten-Free: Most of our paellas are naturally gluten-free. We can identify gluten-free tapas options.
  • Allergies: Please inform us of any allergies when booking, and we will ensure safe preparation.

Please note that all dietary requests must be made at least 7 days prior to your event to ensure proper preparation.
