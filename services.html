Additional Event Information

Venue Accommodations

Our paella catering setup is highly flexible and can be accommodated in a variety of venues, both indoor and outdoor. We bring all necessary equipment, including burners and serving stations. We require a relatively level space for cooking and serving, and access to a standard electrical outlet is helpful but not always necessary depending on the setup. We will coordinate with your venue to ensure a smooth setup and execution.

Catering Menus & Audiovisual

While our focus is on authentic paella, we offer a range of Spanish tapas, salads, and desserts to complement your meal. We can work with you to create a full menu tailored to your event's theme and dietary needs. Please note that while our live cooking provides a visual spectacle, we do not typically provide dedicated audiovisual equipment or services. We can coordinate with your AV provider if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for most events, and 2-3 months for weddings and large corporate events, especially during peak season (May-September).

What is the minimum number of guests you cater for?

Our minimum is typically 10 guests, but we can accommodate smaller groups with an adjusted pricing structure. Please contact us for details.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most allergy-related dietary restrictions with advance notice. Please inform us of any special requirements when booking.

How much space do you need for setup?

We typically need a 10' x 10' area for our cooking station. This can be indoors or outdoors, as long as there is proper ventilation if indoors.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to secure your date, with the balance due one week before the event.
