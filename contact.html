Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 weeks in advance for most events, and 2-3 months for weddings and large corporate events, especially during peak season (May-September).

What is your service area?

We serve the entire state of New Jersey, as well as select areas in New York City, Philadelphia, and Eastern Pennsylvania (additional travel fees may apply).

What is the minimum number of guests?

Our minimum is typically 20 guests, but we can accommodate smaller groups with an adjusted pricing structure.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a $500 deposit to secure your date, with the balance due by the day of the event.
