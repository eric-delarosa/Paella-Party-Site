Our Story

Paella Party NJ was born from Chef Diego De La Rosa's passion for sharing authentic Spanish cuisine with the people of New Jersey. Originally from Cordoba, Spain, Chef Diego brings over 40 years of culinary expertise to every event.

After moving to the United States, Diego and his wife Maria noticed that authentic Spanish paella was hard to find. They decided to start their own catering business 10 years ago, focusing on bringing the true flavors of Spain to special events throughout New Jersey.

What began as a small family operation has grown into a beloved catering service, known for its commitment to authenticity, quality ingredients, and memorable dining experiences.

Meet Chef Diego

Chef Diego De La Rosa

Master Paella Chef & Founder

Born and raised in Cordoba, Spain, Chef Diego developed his culinary skills from an early age, learning traditional recipes passed down through generations of his family. His journey in the culinary world began in the kitchens of Spain's finest restaurants, where he honed his craft and developed a deep appreciation for the rich flavors and techniques of Spanish cuisine.

With over four decades of experience, Chef Diego has mastered the art of paella making. He takes pride in using authentic methods and imported Spanish ingredients, including the finest Bomba rice and genuine Spanish saffron, to create paellas that transport diners straight to the Mediterranean coast.

Chef Diego's passion for sharing his cultural heritage through food is evident in every dish he prepares. He believes that food brings people together and creates lasting memories, which is why he puts his heart and soul into every paella he creates for your special events.

Our Values

Authenticity

We are committed to preserving the authentic flavors and techniques of traditional Spanish paella. From our imported ingredients to our cooking methods, we ensure every dish is a true representation of Spain's culinary heritage.

Family Tradition

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, carrying with them the love and care of family gatherings. We bring this sense of family tradition to every event we cater, treating your guests as we would our own family.

Memorable Experiences

We believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's an experience that creates lasting memories. Our goal is to make your event special with not just delicious food, but also an interactive and engaging culinary display.

Our Cooking Process

1. Premium Ingredients

We start with the finest ingredients, including authentic Spanish Bomba rice, imported saffron, and fresh local produce. Quality ingredients are the foundation of exceptional paella.

2. Traditional Preparation

Following time-honored techniques, we prepare each component of the paella with care, building layers of flavor that will come together in the final dish.

3. Live Cooking

We cook your paella fresh on-site in traditional paella pans, creating an interactive culinary display that engages your guests and builds anticipation.

4. Perfect Socarrat

We carefully monitor the cooking process to achieve the perfect socarrat—the caramelized crust at the bottom of the pan that is the hallmark of authentic paella.

5. Presentation & Service

The finished paella is presented with flair and served to your guests with a side of Spanish hospitality, creating a memorable dining experience.
